About Me

I am a Research Assistant jointly affiliated with the University of Washington and Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET), working on a high-resolution national emission inventory for Bangladesh and supporting air quality modelling and PM₂.₅ monitoring efforts.

I hold a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering with an Environmental specialization from Islamic University of Technology (IUT), graduating with a CGPA of 3.65/4.00. My academic and professional work spans environmental modelling, machine learning for air quality prediction, carbon accounting, microplastics research, and GIS-based infrastructure design.

Research Interests

  • Air quality modelling & national emission inventories
  • PM₂.₅ prediction using machine learning
  • Microplastics removal in water treatment
  • Carbon emissions analysis (GHG Protocol, IPCC, ISO 14064)
  • GIS & remote sensing for environmental applications
  • WASH inequalities in urban informal settlements
