About

I hold a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering with a specialization in Environmental Engineering from Islamic University of Technology (IUT), Bangladesh. My academic and professional work sits at the intersection of environmental monitoring, climate-related analysis, GIS, and data-driven engineering research.

My recent work includes national emission inventory development, PM₂.₅ monitoring and modeling, carbon emissions analysis, ESG-linked intervention assessment, and microplastic removal research.
