Welcome! I am a Research Assistant jointly affiliated with the University of Washington and Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET), working on high-resolution national emission inventories and air quality modelling for Bangladesh.

I hold a B.Sc. in Civil Engineering (Environmental specialization) from Islamic University of Technology (IUT), graduating with a CGPA of 3.65/4.00. My research spans microplastic removal, PM₂.₅ prediction using machine learning, carbon emissions analysis, and GIS-based infrastructure design.

I have published at peer-reviewed international conferences, with a journal submission under review and additional work in progress.
